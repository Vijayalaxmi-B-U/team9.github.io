    Why I Chose the Tourism Sector

    Tourism drives economic growth, creates jobs, and promotes culture. In smart cities, technology enhances tourist experiences by optimizing routes and managing attractions efficiently. This sector demonstrates how smart solutions can make cities more attractive and livable for residents and visitors alike.

    Why I Chose the Tourism Sector

    I have chosen the tourism sector for our smart city design because it brings life, culture, and economic activity to the city. Tourists can explore iconic attractions like the cultural emblem at the center of the lake, Etruna Studio, the filmy city Rajpuri, the majestic Vrindhara Palace, and our national parks. By integrating smart technologies, we can optimize tourist routes, manage attractions efficiently, and provide memorable experiences.
