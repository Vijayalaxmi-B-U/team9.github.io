About

This project focuses on applying algorithmic techniques to improve modern city infrastructure including transportation, resource allocation, and smart automation.

Project Information

Our project explores how algorithms such as Dijkstra, Greedy approaches, and optimization methods can enhance tourism flow, traffic management, smart sensors, and urban decision-making.
